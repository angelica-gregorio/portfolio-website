About Me

I'm Angelica Gregorio, a passionate Computer Engineering and Data Science student with a deep interest in the intersection of hardware and software technologies.

My journey in technology began with embedded systems, where I discovered the power of microcontrollers in creating intelligent solutions. This fascination led me to explore machine learning and data science, enabling me to build systems that not only function but also learn and adapt.

Through my academic projects and personal initiatives, I've developed expertise in embedded systems programming, AI/ML applications, and full-stack web development. I'm particularly interested in projects that have real-world impact, especially in accessibility and smart home technologies.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and sharing knowledge with the tech community.
