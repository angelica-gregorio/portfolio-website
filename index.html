    About Me

    I'm a third-year BS Computer Engineering student with a minor in Data Science at De La Salle University – Manila. I'm honored to be a DOST-SEI R.A. 7687 Scholar, a St. La Salle Financial Assistance Grant Scholar, and a consistent Dean's Lister.

    My core technical passions lie in embedded systems, electronics, and rapid prototyping. I thrive on bringing ideas to life at the hardware-software intersection—whether I'm wiring up microcontrollers for practical security systems or researching offline AI speech interfaces to build accessible, real-world solutions.

    Beyond the breadboard, I am deeply committed to science advocacy and communication. Whether I'm organizing technical seminars with ACCESS DLSU, writing science journalism pieces, or sharing the wonders of space and astronomy through the Skywatchers Society of Las Piñas, my goal is to make science engaging and accessible to everyone.
