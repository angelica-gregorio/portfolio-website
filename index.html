    About Me

    I'm a BS Computer Engineering student at De La Salle University – Manila, maintaining Cum Laude standing. I'm a DOST-SEI R.A. 7687 Scholar, St. La Salle Financial Assistance Grant Scholar, and consistent First Honors Dean's Lister.

    My passion sits at the crossroads of embedded systems and social impact — designing hardware-software solutions that tackle real community problems, from disaster communication hubs using LoRaWAN to assistive devices for people with visual impairments.

    Beyond engineering, I serve as President of ACCESS DLSU, contribute science journalism to campus publications, and champion public astronomy through the Skywatchers Society of Las Piñas.
